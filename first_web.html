Strongest Key Pages in Library of Ruina

What is Library of Ruina?

Library of Ruina is a card deckbuilder indie game. The main gameplay format involves either fighting guests by inviting them to the Library using books you obtain from guests before, or fighing Abnormalities which are monsters that can only be fought to level up floors of the library when you obtain certain books. Once you defeat a guest you obtain their book in which you have the option to burn the book to get combat pages(or cards that you use during fights) and possibly their key pages(which when equipped to one of your librarians give them access to their passives and resistances along with any unique combat pages they have). It should also be noted what floors are in this game, in the Library there are multiple floors with different Patron Libarians which are the first characters you are able to use when you first unlock the floor and you need to gain more librarians by leveling up said floor. The floors are the Floor of general works, History,Technological Sciences,Literature, Art, Natural Sciences, Language, Social Sciences, Philosophy and Religion. The Patron Libarians for these floors are respicatively Roland, Malkuth, Yesod, Hod, Netzach, Tiphereth, Gebura, Chesed, Binah and Hokma which are names based off the Tree of Life except for Roland for reasons I shall not state for now. Now for more gameplay aspects I should explain is Light which is the resource that you make your libarians use to use combat pages, of course some require 0 and some 5 and since light is so important you should run at least 1 combat page that restores light, you can also getr light by emotion levels. Before I explain emotion levels I'm going to explain what aare dices, so dices are what are used when you use a combat pages and there are three types of dices which are combat dices which have subcatagories like blunt, slash or pierce combat dices which have no speical effects besides either doing more damage or stagger damage(Enemies and allies alike have a stagger bar) to enemies or less based on their resistances, block dices which will lessen damage if they lose a clash(when two dices go against each other) or deal stagger damage based on the difference between the two rolls, evade dices which when they roll higher will ignore the attacking dice and recover stagger based on evade roll and be reused for the next attack hwoever if it rolls less the user will take damage and the evade dice reuse will not work if it clashes with a block dice, and lastly there are counter dices which only activate upon a one sided clash where the user of the counter dice will use the dice against the one sided attack and reuse it until the one sided attack is over or if it loses a clash(something to note is that these dices will not be reused again on another one sided attack unless it kills the enemy it was beforehand). There's also emotion levels which are gained when certain emotion point threshold, emotion points are gained whenever a character wins or loses a clash or when they roll the maximum or minimum values of a dice, there are two types of emotion points which are positive and negative coins which are gained depending if the action that made them get the emotion point was good or bad for them. When a emotion level is reached the respective character will get a buff and for the libarians if all of them reach a certain emotion level then abnormality pages(basically a buff that are based on the Abnormalities you have currently fought on the floor) can be given to one libarian, all of them or all characters and there are two types of these pages which are Awakening pages(buffs with no drawbacks) and Breakdown pages(stronger buff at the cost of a drawback). Later on in the game you get access to E.G.O pages which are strong attacks based off Abnormalities gained through these emotion levels

Red Mist Key Page

The Red Mist key page is obtainable by completing the reception of the Red Mist and can only be equipped by Gebura in the floor of Language. The reasons for this is that the Red Mist is what Gebura used to be in the past. The passives of this key page are Legendary, Retaliate, The Strongest and Speed III. The first passive, Legendary, makes it so only Gebura can equip the page and that a unique E.G.O becomes avaliable at emotion level 4 or above which is gained through the use of a special combat page of Manifest E.G.O. The second passive, Retaliate, makes it so Gebura always has a counter dice avaliable by giving her a strong counter dice at all times. The third passive, The Strongest, makes it so at every turn the dice with the lowest speed value will become a infinite speed dice or the maximum value. The fourth passive isn't anything note worthy because all it does is just give her a extra speed dice to use combat pages on at all times. Now the strength of this key page comes from her unique pages which are Great Split: Vertical, Great Split: Horizontal, Focus Spirit, Level Slash, Upstanding Slash, Spear and Onrush. Greater Split: Vertical and Horizontal I'll discuss at the same time, they are unique combat pages worth 5 and 6 light specifically, Greater Split: Vertical is a strong single dice that rolls 20-39 and has the unique effect on hit of destroying a combat page on another speed dice the person that got hit by it, Greater Split: Horizontal on the other hand rolls even higher as it rolls 28-42 along with being a Mass Attack page(A page that attacks all targets that rolled less than the value the mass attack rolled) but however can only be usuable when the Red Mist E.G.O is activated. To quickly discuss the unique E.G.O is that when activated will increase the roll of all dices by 2 but takes stagger damage when Gebura does less than 40 damage that turn and is lost when Gebura is staggered. Focus Spirit is a at first lackluster page having a 8-12 block dice then using a 5-7 blunt dice and costing 2 light to use this page, but this page makes Gebura gain 3 strength(boosts the rolls of all dice by how mujch strength is on user when gained for that turn) when the blunt dice hits. The next three combat pages have similar mechanics of rolling pretty high and only one specific type of combat dice(Level slash does slash damage, Spear does pierce damage and Upstanding Slash does Blunt damage) and they cost 2 light which may seem expensive at first however they all have the mechanic of lowering all copies of their respicative pages after dealing 8+ damage however Spear and Upstanding Slash of drawing one page while Level Slash restores one light as well when the 8+ damage threshold is met. Onrush is an interesting page as it only has a single strong slash dice rolling 14-26 for 3 light, however the dice will be reused on a random enemy if the enemy it had attacked beforehand had been staggered or killed. The Red Mist's page strength shows with how high her single target damage is along with her potential AOE damage, and how quick she's able to ramp up due to how her main pages who will use have special effects once dealing enough damage. Passives you may want to add to her page are specifically ones that boost power like Myo's Prowess which reads When using a Combat Page, all dice on the page gain Power against targets with slower Speed (+1 Power per 2 points of difference, up to 5) and or Grace of the Prescript which boosts the power of a random damage by 2.
